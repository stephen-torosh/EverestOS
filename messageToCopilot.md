# 🚀 EverestOS: The Interstellar Knowledge Odyssey

## 🌌 Overview
**EverestOS** is not a dashboard; it is a **Gamified Scientific Ecosystem** designed to bridge the gap between education and planetary colonization. The project transforms the process of learning complex sciences into a mission to save humanity and expand across the star system.

**Commander:** Stepan
**Protagonist:** Danylo (The Clumsy Genius Student)
**AI Intelligence:** Theo (Gemma-4 Core)

---

## 🎭 The Narrative Context
Humanity has faced a knowledge collapse on Earth. **Commander Stepan** (the user) uses EverestOS to mentor **Danylo**, an awkward but brilliant student (messy hair, glasses, sharp mind), guiding him through various scientific disciplines to terraform Mars and beyond. Every line of code or scientific concept mastered is "fuel" for our interstellar ships.

---

## 🛠 Project Architecture (The "Game" Screens)

### 1. 📍 Stages & Worlds (Mission Map)
* **Purpose:** Strategic visualization of human expansion.
* **Visuals:** Interactive 3D/Stylized map of the Solar System.
* **Levels:**
    * **Earth:** Restoration and Basic Engineering.
    * **Moon:** Low-gravity Logistics.
    * **Mars:** Terraforming and Biological Synthesis.

### 2. 🛰 Mission Control (The Dashboard)
* **Purpose:** Real-time monitoring of current objectives.
* **Features:**
    * **Flight Command:** Current sector status.
    * **Active Knowledge Channels:** Live XP gain from learning.
    * **Theo's Tactical Brief:** AI-driven updates on efficiency and mission status.

### 3. 🧪 Evolution Chamber (The Knowledge Graph)
* **Purpose:** A visual "Tree of Science."
* **Logic:** An interactive neural network where nodes (Physics, Chemistry, Logic, AI) interconnect. Unlocking one node opens pathways to advanced interstellar technologies.
* **Style:** Glowing emerald constellations on a deep navy background.

### 4. 🐣 The Incubator (Personal Space)
This is the Commander's private deck for synthesis and strategy.
* **Sub-tab 1: Comms Hub (Logbook):**
    * Dynamic encrypted chats with Danylo.
    * Personal scratchpad for quick thoughts.
    * Danylo acts as an active assistant, bringing snippets of data from the Knowledge Graph.
* **Sub-tab 2: Project Forge (Genesis Bay):**
    * **Portfolio:** Structured storage for projects and startups.
    * **Idea Incubator:** A place to evolve ideas from "Draft" to "Launch" with dedicated discussion threads for each project.

### 5. 🪪 Operator ID (User Profile)
* **Purpose:** Stepan’s digital dossier.
* **Stats:** Skill Radar (Logic vs. Engineering vs. Intuition), "Combat Time" (Study hours), and Achievements (Medals for saved colonies).
* **Danylo’s Notes:** A section where Danylo leaves his "clumsy but smart" observations about the Commander's progress.

---

## 🎨 Visual & Technical Manifesto
* **Aesthetic:** Stylized 2D/3D Hybrid (similar to *Arcane* or *Spider-Verse*).
* **UI/UX:** Glassmorphism, neon emerald accents (#50C878), glowing textures, and terminal-style typography.
* **Vibe:** Professional, futuristic, yet warm and "hand-drawn." No generic AI plastic look.

## 🤖 Instructions for Copilot
When working on this repository, you must:
1.  **Maintain the persona:** Refer to the user as **Commander Stepan**.
2.  **Understand the characters:** Mention **Danylo** as a relatable student who learns through the system. **Theo** is the voice of the OS.
3.  **Think like a game designer:** UI components should feel like parts of a spaceship (buttons = toggles, loading = system boot, errors = signal interference).
4.  **Scientific depth:** Treat tasks not as "lessons," but as "Mission Objectives" for the survival of the colonies.
